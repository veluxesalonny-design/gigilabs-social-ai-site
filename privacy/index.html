Privacy Policy

Last updated: 22 July 2026. This policy explains how GIGILABS collects, uses, stores and protects information when you use our website, AI production services and authorized social-media integrations.

1. Who we are2. Information collected3. Social integrations4. How we use data5. Sharing6. Retention7. Security8. Your rights9. Children10. Changes11. Contact
GIGILABS does not ask for or store your TikTok, Facebook, Instagram, YouTube, X, LinkedIn or other social-media passwords. Connections use the platform's official authorization process.

1. Who we are

GIGILABS is an AI and digital production studio providing AI-assisted content creation, websites, applications, branding, automation, social-media workflows and product-launch services. For privacy questions, contact gigilabs4@gmail.com.

2. Information we collect

Information you provide

  • Your name, email address, company name and project details.
  • Content, media, prompts, captions, instructions and files you submit for production.
  • Messages, approvals and support communications.

Technical information

  • Basic device, browser, IP address, security and usage information generated when you use our website or services.
  • Logs needed to protect the service, diagnose errors and prevent abuse.

Connected-account information

When you connect a third-party platform, we may receive the minimum account identifiers, profile information, permissions and access tokens that the platform makes available and that you authorize. The exact information depends on the platform and approved scopes.

3. TikTok and other social-media integrations

For TikTok, GIGILABS Social AI may request permissions such as user.info.basic, video.upload and video.publish. These permissions are used to:

  • Identify the TikTok account that you chose to connect.
  • Upload a video that you selected or approved.
  • Publish content only after you review the final media, caption, privacy setting and destination.

GIGILABS does not publish to an account that has not authorized access. We do not use connected-account data to build advertising profiles or sell personal information.

You can revoke access at any time through the connected platform's security settings or by contacting us. Once access is revoked, future publishing stops. We will delete or de-identify retained integration data when it is no longer required for security, legal or operational purposes.

4. How we use information

  • To provide, customize and improve requested services.
  • To generate, edit, review, schedule or publish content you approve.
  • To communicate about projects, support, security and service changes.
  • To maintain records of consent, approvals and authorized publishing actions.
  • To prevent fraud, abuse, unauthorized access and technical failures.
  • To comply with applicable laws, platform rules and lawful requests.

5. How information is shared

We may share information with trusted service providers that help us host, secure, generate, process or deliver the service. These providers may include cloud-hosting, AI, analytics, email, payment and social-platform providers. We share only what is reasonably necessary for the relevant task.

We may also disclose information when legally required, to protect rights and safety, or in connection with a genuine business reorganization. We do not sell personal information.

6. Data retention

We keep information only for as long as reasonably necessary to provide the service, maintain security, resolve disputes, meet legal obligations and preserve approved project records. Retention periods vary by data type and project. Temporary media or publishing credentials may be removed sooner.

7. Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical and organizational safeguards, including access controls, secure connections and restricted handling of credentials. No online service can guarantee absolute security, so users should also protect their own accounts and promptly report suspected misuse.

8. Your choices and rights

Subject to applicable law, you may request access, correction, deletion, restriction or a copy of personal information associated with you. You may also withdraw consent where processing is based on consent. Send requests to gigilabs4@gmail.com. We may need to verify your identity before completing a request.

How to disconnect or delete integration data

  1. Revoke GIGILABS access from the social platform's connected-app or security settings.
  2. Email gigilabs4@gmail.com with the subject “Data deletion request”.
  3. Identify the connected account without sending any password or secret token.

9. Children

Our business services are not directed to children under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Contact us if you believe a child has provided information without appropriate authorization.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy to reflect service, legal or platform changes. The updated date will appear at the top of this page. Material changes may also be communicated through the service or by email.
